History

  • Settled in 1851 by pioneers of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints
  • Name "Spanish Fork" was derived from the fact that the route of the Taos trappers during the early part of the 1800's followed the canyon and the river
  • 1862: First school built
  • 1909: First lighty and power system built
  • 1925: First annual Utah County livestock show held in City Square
