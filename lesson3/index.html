Site Name:

Idaho Weather

The site name is Idaho Weather, which I have chosen because it is simple and straight to the point in what the site's objective is.

Purpose:

The purpose of Idaho Weather is to help the citizens and visitors of Idaho know the weather of the state 24/7, with the information provided on this site, people can know every week what the weather will be like and know how to plan their days accordingly.

Domain:

www.idahoweather.com

This domain is easy and simple and has no spaces or special characters.
